How to Play

  1. Rotate the Planet with Left Click + Drag to navigate the planet, also use the mouse wheel scroll.
  2. Move your cursor over the planet's surface to scan nearby sources. The HUD shows latitude, longitude, depth, and material readout.
  3. The water that you need to obtain gives off a distinct wave signature that you can observe in the top right. You can also hear what TOXIN/WASTE sounds like too!
  4. Try to obtain water while minimizing the amount of waste pickup.
